Opening Range Momentum / Reversal

Deployed

Exploits directional bias in the first intraday bar across two timeframes. Brute-force grid search over 3.4M+ trade events, narrowed to 11 statistically robust configs via bootstrap CI filtering and outlier-dependence analysis. Currently trading live.

Rejection Candle + RSI Extremes

Edge Found

Combines candlestick rejection patterns with RSI extreme readings as a mean-reversion signal. 9 tickers pass across 97 tested, with +9.1 bps net EV (p = 0.0001) and 57% win rate on out-of-sample data.

Rejection Candle (Standalone)

No Edge

Tests whether dominant-wick candles (institutional rejection) predict direction. Scanned 3,600+ gate combinations across 99 tickers. Mean test EV = −0.04R; 96% of configs fail. The signal lacks directional content without additional context.

Mean-Reversion Dip

No Edge

Detects price pullbacks from recent highs at various lookback windows and reversion thresholds. 95% of configs fail across 99 tickers; transaction costs kill the marginal mean-reversion signal.

Fair Value Gap Pairs

No Edge

Tests whether consecutive fair value gaps (same or opposite direction) predict price continuation or reversal. Analyzed 25.8M FVG pairs across 41 tickers. MFE/MAE ratio = 0.999 — perfectly symmetric, zero directional content.

Liquidity Sweep

No Edge

Tests wick sweeps at structural price levels (prior-day high/low, opening range) using a paired-delta methodology against matched controls. 2.5M+ large-cap observations yield zero statistical significance — sweeps are real events but not directionally predictive.

OU Mean-Reversion Rules

No Edge

Rule-based mean-reversion inspired by Ornstein–Uhlenbeck processes. Iterated through 3+ research phases with progressively refined entry gates and exit logic. All configs are negative EV at realistic transaction costs.

VWAP Crossover

No Edge

Tests VWAP crossing signals with divergence thresholds. Full grid of 468 signal configurations × 1,344 exit parameters across 41 tickers. No post-cost edge found.

MIROS

Medical Image to Reduced-Order Simulation

End-to-end pipeline that produces patient-specific hemodynamic simulations directly from volumetric angiography in minutes. Integrates ML-based segmentation (SeqSeg) with 0D/1D Navier–Stokes solvers in SimVascular, including automatic boundary condition tuning via CMA-ES optimization.

Highlights

  • Automated pipeline from medical images to blood flow simulation results
  • Automatic boundary condition tuning via CMA-ES optimization
  • 1D Navier–Stokes + 0D lumped-parameter solvers
  • Interactive GUI for cardiac inflow waveform design
  • Cross-platform support (Windows, Linux, macOS)
