Boyang (Bryan) Gan

PhD Student, Columbia University

I am a computational researcher working at the intersection of biomechanics, scientific computing, and machine learning to build digital twins of the human heart. My work spans cardiac electrophysiology, tissue mechanics, and circulatory modeling, with the broader aim of supporting prevention, treatment, and disease remodeling. Outside the lab, I enjoy building systematic trading strategies, playing ultimate frisbee, and cooking.
